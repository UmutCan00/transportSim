Overview

Industries produce raw materials, processors transform them into goods, and neighborhoods and other consumers pay you to receive deliveries. Connect everything with roads, rail, ships, and planes to build a growing logistics empire.

Industries are grouped into city clusters spread across the map. Build road networks within each city for local hauls — then use trains, ships, or planes for long-distance bulk transport between cities.

Controls

  • Pan: Click and drag on empty map
  • Zoom: Scroll wheel
  • Build: Select a tool, then left-click (or drag)
  • Inspect: Click on any tile or building with no tool active

Getting Started

  • 1. Place a Depot somewhere near the center of your first city
  • 2. Build Roads connecting industries you want to link (drag to paint)
  • 3. Place Stations adjacent to industries — they auto-link to the nearest one
  • 4. In the Depot panel, create a Route: pick a pickup station and a dropoff station
  • 5. Buy a Truck and assign it to that route — money starts flowing

Supply Chains

Each chain ends at a final consumer that pays per delivery. Set up the full chain for maximum income:

Coal Mine → Coal → Power Plant
Coal Mine → Coal → Steel Mill → Steel → Factory → Goods → Neighborhood
Iron Mine → Iron → Smelter → Steel → Factory → Goods → Neighborhood
Forest → Wood → Sawmill → Goods → Neighborhood
Oil Well → Oil → Refinery → Goods → Neighborhood
Oil Well → Oil → Chem Plant → Chemicals → Chem Distributor
Farm → Grain → Bakery → Food → Market

Processors (Sawmill, Factory, etc.) need both an input delivery route AND an output pickup route, or they'll fill up and block everything upstream.

Long-Distance Transport

  • 🚛 Trucks — Low cost, fast setup. Best for intra-city short hauls.
  • 🚂 Trains — 3× truck capacity, requires Railway tech + rail tracks. Ideal for high-volume inter-city routes.
  • ✈ Planes — Fly direct (no roads), fast. Requires Aviation tech + airports on both ends.
  • ⛵ Ships — Massive capacity (80 units), very slow. Requires Maritime tech + seaports on coast tiles.
💡 Tip: Build trucks first to fund the Railway tech — then switch inter-city routes to trains for 3× profit.

Tech Tree

Open the 🔬 Tech panel to spend money on upgrades. Recommended order:

  • Cheaper Roads — cuts road cost by 30%
  • Faster Trucks — +30% truck speed
  • Larger Stations — doubles station cargo capacity
  • Railway Engineering — unlocks trains & rail tracks
  • Aviation / Maritime — unlocks planes and ships

Locked Neighborhoods

Some city clusters start 🔒 locked. Click on a locked neighborhood to see its unlock cost. Once unlocked, the whole cluster opens up — adding new consumers and raw producers worth connecting.

Objectives & Scoring

Complete Objectives (shown in the 🎯 panel) for bonus cash. They range from completing your first delivery to hauling hundreds of units of specific cargo. Completing objectives early funds your expansion.

💡 Tip: Each city has one of every industry type. You don't need to haul inter-city to make money — but trains/ships between cities make far more per trip.
